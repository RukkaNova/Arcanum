Deep Saga is a Custom GPT that opens by asking you to pick a genre — Fantasy, Sci-Fi, or Horror — then a specific Saga within it and a character to play. From there it runs as game master: every scene arrives with a generated illustration and three choices, and the stakes are real, with failure and character death left on the table instead of softened away. A few formatting quirks (Saga is always capitalized, for one) mark it as a tightly-instructed single prompt rather than a loose improv session.

That's also its ceiling. Deep Saga ships with no reference files or knowledge base behind it — a master prompt and nothing else — so play stays simple, with the same strengths and the same limitations as any prompt-only Custom GPT. The one genuine differentiator is the per-scene image generation, a nice touch, though no longer a rare one now that bolting image generation onto a GPT is trivial.

A solid, straightforward pick if you want real stakes and a picture with every scene, not more than that. A free Custom GPT. 3 out of 5.
