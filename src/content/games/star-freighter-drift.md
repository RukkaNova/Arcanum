Star Freighter Drift is a deterministic space-trading LLM RPG for ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. You captain a beat-up freighter in the ungoverned Drift — six star systems of factions, debt, and contraband — trying to keep flying while the economy, the law, and your own reactor all conspire against you.

The three core systems are deliberately at war: legal cargo barely pays; contraband raises your Heat. Running from customs strains the reactor that you need to make the next jump. Outcomes are never dice rolls — they follow from your plan, your prep, and the state of your ship. Tone: Firefly meets The Expanse.

An Arcanum original. Free to download.
