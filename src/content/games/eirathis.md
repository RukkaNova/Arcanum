Eirathis Strider is a mature, open-world LLM RPG built for Gemini Gems. You play a Strider — a free wanderer with no lord and no nation — roaming a world split across three overlapping layers of reality, taking work from factions who will hire you or hunt you, and building relationships with companions who pursue their own lives in the background.

Combat is dice-free: success depends entirely on your skill tier, the intelligence of your tactic, and what you prepared beforehand. Skills grow from Novice to Mythic through use, shown in the narration rather than a stat sheet. The tone draws from *The Witcher* and *BG3* — adult, morally grey, and built for players who want a text RPG with real weight.

An Arcanum original. Free to download.
