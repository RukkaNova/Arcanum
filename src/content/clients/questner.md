Questner is a browser-based AI RPG built around a pace we rarely see in this category: no downtime, almost no travel, and measurable progress on every single turn. A session drops you straight into a loop of combat, quest turn-ins, market runs, and barracks upgrades, one after another, with a new power spike every few actions rather than every few sessions.

That makes it a clear outlier against the deep, [tabletop-shaped](/clients/friends-and-fables) and [sandbox-style](/clients/deep-realms) AI RPGs we cover most. Questner reads as a casual, low-time-commitment action RPG — built for a phone or tablet and a spare hour, not a long campaign.
