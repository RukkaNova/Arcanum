AI Game Master — Dungeon RPG is a mobile text RPG for iOS and Android (a web version is listed as in development) that blends D&D-inspired elements with GPT-driven storytelling: create a hero, pick a genre, and an AI game master narrates your adventure with image generation along the way. The concept is familiar — because it is, in almost every particular, the AI Dungeon concept.

## How It Works

Each action you take costs one token. The free tier grants 5 tokens every 4 hours across 5 story slots; paid tiers raise the allowance ($15/month for 500 tokens, $25/month for 1,000) up to an unlimited plan. Genres range from dark fantasy to sci-fi, characters carry stats, skills, and inventory, and the AI generates scene art alongside the prose.

## Advantages Over Competitors

We looked, honestly. The genre switching is easy, the character setup is serviceable, and if you specifically want a phone-native AI Dungeon-alike, it exists on your phone. That is the list.

## What It Does Best (and Worst)

At its best, it is a functional freeform mobile story engine for short sessions. At its worst — which is most of the time when compared against alternatives — it feels like a lower-production-value version of [AI Dungeon](/clients/ai-dungeon): same freeform loop, same standard-context memory limits, rougher writing, rougher interface, and a token meter running underneath all of it.

## Limitations

The production values are the core problem, and they extend to the platform's own storefront: at the time of review, the official pricing page still contained literal placeholder text ("Add terms and conditions") alongside multiple misspellings — "Entuhsiast," "Unlimted," "Sequals" — and two different tiers priced identically at $25 with wildly different allowances. None of that is disqualifying by itself, but it tells you how much polish to expect inside. Memory behaves like a standard context window, with the drift and forgetting that implies on longer stories.

## Verdict

Every platform in this directory gets the same question: who should choose this over the alternatives? For AI Game Master, we could not construct an answer. If you want freeform AI storytelling, AI Dungeon does it with more polish at comparable prices; if you want structure, half this directory does it better. Rough, redundant, and outclassed. 1 out of 5.
